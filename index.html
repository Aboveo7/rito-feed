About HUNCH

HUNCH is an independent production house built on instinct, patience, and a deep respect for storytelling. We believe that powerful stories don’t rely on noise — they rely on intention. Every project developed under HUNCH begins with a clear emotional core and is shaped carefully through visuals, pacing, and atmosphere.

Our work is driven by creators who value process over speed. From experimenting with digital worlds and game engines to blending cinematic language with modern tools, HUNCH exists to explore new ways of telling stories while staying grounded in narrative discipline.

With Silent Brother as our first long-form cinematic vision, HUNCH marks a focused chapter — one where storytelling comes first, visuals serve emotion, and every frame carries purpose. This is not content made to be rushed. This is work built to last.
